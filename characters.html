The Characters

The Professor

The Professor is of above-average height, with somewhat curly, medium-long and dark hair. He has brown eyes, as well as a beard and glasses. He is well groomed and is usually seen sporting a grey suit along with a black tie and white shirt.

Berlin

Andrés de Fonollosa, better known by his codename Berlin, is a character in the Money Heist series, appearing as one of the main characters. He is the second-in-command of the robbers of the Royal Mint of Spain.

Tokyo

Silene Oliveira, better known by her code-name Tokyo, is one of the main protagonists of the Netflix series Money Heist, as well as narrator of the story. She is a member of the heist crew set to rob the Royal Mint of Spain. Knows how to use guns and shit but not her own brain.

Rio

Anníbal Cortés, also known by his code-name Rio, was an expert hacker and a member of a group who robbed the Royal Mint of Spain. Why is he on the team again?

Monica

Mónica Gaztambide worked at the Royal Mint of Spain. She had an affair with Arturo Román, her director and boss. After the great robbery, she joins the robbers and marries Denver, whom she raises her child with. She is given the alias "Stockholm".

Raquel

Raquel Murillo, later known by her alias Lisbon, was a Spanish police inspector. She was tasked with leading the police team at the First Robbery, targeting the Royal Mint of Spain. During the robbery, she falls for The Professor, and flees with him at the end of Season 2.

Palermo

Rodrigo de la Serna portrayed the role of Martin Berotte (also known as Palermo) is known for being the mastermind behind the heist on the National Bank of Spain.
